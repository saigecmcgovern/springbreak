Sleeping arrangements

This task will tell you how to find the place you are sleeping and make the bed.

I will be sleeping at home.

To get to your room and make the bed:

  1. Go down the stairs to the basement. Once downstairs, open the first door straight ahead. This is your room.
  2. There will be a pile of sheets and two pillows on the bed. Separate them and make sure there is a loose sheet, a fitted sheet, and two pillowcases.
  3. Put the fitted sheet on the bed. Yell out if you need help.
  4. Put the loose sheet on over it and fold it down.
  5. Put the pillows inside the pillowcases.
  6. Go into the closet. From the top shelf, take the comforter and place it on the bed over the sheets. Your bed is now made.
  7. At the end of the week, put all of the sheets, pillowcases, and the comforter in the laundry room to be washed.
