Things to do

This task will explain what we are going to do over spring break when we are not sleeping or eating.

I will be spending all of my time at work.

To get ready and get to my work:

  1. Put on khakis, the collared uniform shirt, your nametag, and non-slip shoes. Make sure you have your hat and cutting glove in case they want you to work in the kitchen.
  2. Get in the car and take 7-East to the Leesburg Village.
  3. Take the first right after the exit. You are now in the parking lot. Park, and go into the store.
  4. Go directly to the back room and clock in. Try to avoid customers as they will want to talk to you and will make you late to clock in.
  5. Check the whiteboard next to the timeclock to see what you are doing.
  6. Consult a manager for further steps once you have your assignment.
  7. At the end of the day, clock out and take 7-West to get home.
  8. Park your car in the driveway, go inside, and take a nap until you smell dinner.
