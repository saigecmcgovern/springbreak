Help: Traveling

You are low on gas.

You forgot to get gas before leaving.

Now, you are stuck either on Route 7 or 81 and need to find a safe place to stop.

Here is how you can make sure you stay safe while getting gas.

  • Determine how long you can wait to get gas, and scout out available exits.
  • The exits with the most restaurants, gas stations, and businesses are the safest, since they have the most people around and therefore more witnesses.
  • Only go to well-lit areas, and try not to get too far off the main road.
  • If you must leave your car unattended, be sure to keep all perosnal belongings with you and lock the doors.
  • If you feel unsafe, trust your instinct and go somewher else.
